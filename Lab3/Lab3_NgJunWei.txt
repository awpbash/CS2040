Coconut.java

have n players with n hands at first
phrase with S syllabus is played
queue has length n at the start

if lands on folded arms, the folded arms is removed and 2 fists are added to the FRONT

if land on fist, the fist is turn to palms down and added to the BACK

if land on palms down, the hand is placed at the back and REMOVED

if both hands of player is at the back, they are out of game

sample queue = [folded(1), folded(2)]
1st run
[fist(2), fist(2), folded(1)]
etc
